Hi, my name is Debashis Barman. I'm a 20 year old college guy who is currently doing Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at Assam Don Bosco University in Guwahati, India. I have been addicted to the Internet since I was young and started making websites since I was in 12. I'm mostly interested in front end web designing but also otherwise enjoy any kind of programming (specially C and Kernel programming) and web development works.

I try to make the websites I design and build as simple and intuitive to use as possible whilst still having a unique aesthetic appeal. I specialise in responsive front-end design, HTML/CSS and web typography.

When I'm free, I spend my time exploring places and beyond, adventuring in the wilderness, learning about the world, working on tiny side projects, writing, romancing my lovely girlfriend, sketching and doodling, reading short stories and novels, listening to music and playing games. And internet. I love internet.
